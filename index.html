OUR SERVICES

Comprehensive Aluminum Repair Solutions

We offer a complete range of professional services designed to restore and enhance your aluminum components with precision and expertise.

Damage Assessment

Our expert technicians conduct thorough inspections of your aluminum components using advanced diagnostic techniques. We identify all visible and hidden damage, assess structural integrity, and determine the most effective repair approach to restore your equipment to optimal condition.

  • Non-destructive testing methods
  • Detailed damage documentation
  • Comprehensive repair recommendations

Weld Repair & Reconstruction

We specialize in precision welding and reconstruction of damaged aluminum components. Our certified welders use state-of-the-art equipment and techniques to create strong, durable repairs that meet or exceed original specifications. Every weld is inspected for quality assurance.

  • TIG and MIG welding capabilities
  • Certified welders with extensive experience
  • Quality assurance testing

Surface Finishing

Professional surface finishing completes the restoration process, ensuring your aluminum components look and perform like new. We offer multiple finishing options including polishing, anodizing, and protective coatings that enhance appearance and provide long-lasting protection.

  • Multiple finishing options available
  • Corrosion-resistant coatings
  • Aesthetic enhancement

ABOUT DUBYA LLC

Trusted Partner in Aluminum Restoration

Founded in 2015, Dubya LLC emerged from a passion for precision engineering and sustainable manufacturing practices. Our founder, Sam, recognized a critical gap in the market for high-quality aluminum repair services that could extend component lifespan while reducing environmental waste. What began as a small workshop has grown into a trusted regional leader, serving hundreds of satisfied clients across diverse industries including aerospace, marine, automotive, and industrial manufacturing.

Our mission is simple yet powerful: to deliver exceptional aluminum repair and restoration services that combine technical expertise, innovative solutions, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. We believe in the value of restoration over replacement, both economically and environmentally. Every project we undertake reflects our core values of integrity, quality, and continuous improvement. We're not just fixing aluminum—we're preserving valuable assets, supporting sustainable practices, and building lasting relationships with our clients through transparency, reliability, and outstanding results.

Common Questions Answered

Everything you need to know about our aluminum repair and restoration services

We repair a comprehensive range of aluminum damage including cracks, dents, warping, corrosion, and structural damage. Our expertise covers everything from minor surface imperfections to major weld repairs and complete reconstructions. Whether your aluminum components have suffered impact damage, environmental deterioration, or operational wear, our team has the skills and equipment to restore them to optimal condition. We work with various aluminum alloys and can handle both simple repairs and complex restoration projects.

Most repairs are completed within 5-10 business days from the initial assessment. The exact timeline depends on the complexity and scope of the damage, the size of the component, and our current project queue. Simple repairs like minor dent removal or surface finishing might be completed in 2-3 days, while more complex weld repairs or reconstructions may take longer. We always provide a detailed timeline estimate after our initial assessment. We understand that downtime is costly for your business, so we prioritize efficient turnaround without compromising quality.

Typically, our repair services cost 40-60% less than purchasing replacement components. The exact savings depend on your specific component, the extent of damage, and current market prices for new equipment. Beyond the direct cost savings, you also benefit from reduced downtime (repairs are typically faster than sourcing and installing replacements), avoiding inventory costs, and minimizing disruption to your operations. For many businesses, especially those with specialized or hard-to-source components, the savings can be substantial. We provide detailed cost comparisons and transparent pricing estimates so you can make an informed decision.

Our warranty covers any defects or issues related to our repair work. We stand behind our quality and are committed to your satisfaction. If any issues arise during the warranty period, we'll address them promptly at no additional cost. Additionally, we offer a satisfaction guarantee—if you're not completely satisfied with the results, we'll work with you to make it right.
